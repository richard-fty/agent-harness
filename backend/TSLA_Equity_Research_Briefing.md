## Fundamentals & News

### Q1 2025 Deliveries
- Tesla reported **336,000 deliveries** in Q1 2025, down **13%** from the 387,000 delivered in Q1 2024.
- Despite the year-over-year decline, the company posted **16% revenue growth** for the quarter, helped by higher average selling prices and regulatory credit sales.
- A **$25 billion capital spending plan** for 2026 has drawn scrutiny from analysts concerned about cash flow pressure.

### Key Catalysts to Watch
1. **Full Self-Driving (FSD) expansion** — Regulatory approvals for unsupervised FSD in new markets
2. **Cybertruck ramp** — Production scaling and demand trajectory
3. **Next-gen platform** — Updates on the lower-cost vehicle platform expected in 2026-2027
4. **Energy business** — Tesla's energy storage and solar segment continues to grow rapidly
5. **$25B capex plan** — Investors watching for ROI on massive capital deployment
