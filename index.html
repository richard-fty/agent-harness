Data Notes
• Revenue: Total transaction value in USD.
• Gross Margin: (Revenue − Cost of Goods Sold) ÷ Revenue, as a decimal.
• Units: Number of individual items/services sold.
• Weighted avg margin = Σ(revenue × margin) ÷ Σ(revenue) across the filtered set.
• Dataset covers Jan–Dec 2025 across 4 regions (North America, EMEA, APAC, LATAM) and 5 categories (Platform, Subscriptions, Hardware, Services, Training).
